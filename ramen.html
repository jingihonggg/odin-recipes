Making Instant Ramen: A poor man's guide to a delicious heart warming bowl of RAMEN!!!

Again, it isn't too difficult. Breathe. It'll be alright.

    Again, you can mix and match or add whatever you want honestly in terms of meats and vegetables. The broth is going to most likely make it taste good anyway.

  • Can of Tuna
  • Raw Eggs
  • Green Onions
  • Mushrooms

    STEPS:

  1. 1. Get a pot and fill it with water. Bring it to a boil
  2. 2. Once the water is boiling, put the packet contents in it including the dried vegetable flakes and broth spice.
  3. 3. Give it a stir and then put in the fried noodles along with your green onions and mushrooms.
  4. 4. Once the noodles start getting a bit softer, crack a couple of raw eggs in there (or as many as you like) and don't fully cook it because slightly raw eggs taste divine.
  5. 5. Optional: Eat with kimchi.
