Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich Recipe

I'm going to teach you how to make a peanut butter and jelly sandwich today

Trust me, it's really not that hard

    Ingredients:

  • peanut butter
  • jelly
  • bread

    Steps:

  1. *optional*: toast the slice(s) of bread
  2. Grab as many slices of bread as you want. I usually go with two.
  3. Next grab two knives or two spoons: one for each jar (peanut butter and jelly containers).
  4. Next, apply as much peanut butter on one slice of bread (or on half if using one slice) and apply as much jelly as you want to the other.
  5. Carefully...put the slices of bread together and enjoy with a glass of milk!
